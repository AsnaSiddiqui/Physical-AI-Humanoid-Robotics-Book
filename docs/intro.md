# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook will guide you through the fundamental concepts to advanced applications, with a focus on practical implementation using ROS 2, Gazebo, Unity, and NVIDIA Isaac Sim.

## Book Objectives

- Understand the core principles of Physical AI
- Learn to build and control humanoid robots
- Master simulation techniques for robotics
- Integrate AI systems with robotic platforms
- Implement vision-language-action systems

## Target Audience

This book is designed for students, researchers, and developers interested in robotics, AI, and humanoid robotics systems, ranging from beginners to intermediate professionals.
